Our 3-Step Responsible Disposal Process

Every load we collect goes through a meticulous sorting process designed to maximize reuse and recycling while minimizing landfill impact.

1

Initial Sorting & Assessment

Upon arrival at our Ventura County sorting facility, every item undergoes initial assessment. Our trained staff immediately identifies:

  • Items suitable for local donation
  • Materials eligible for specialized recycling
  • Products that require special handling (e-waste, hazardous materials)
  • Items that must go to proper landfill facilities
2

Destination Routing

Items are separated into specific streams based on their next destination. This careful routing ensures:

  • Donation-quality items reach local charities quickly
  • Recyclables are sent to appropriate facilities
  • Specialty items like mattresses and e-waste get proper handling
  • Minimal contamination between different waste streams
3

Verification & Reporting

We track where materials end up and can provide transparency about the final destinations. Our commitment includes:

  • Partnership verification with donation centers
  • Recycling facility certifications
  • Landfill diversion rate tracking
  • Continuous improvement of our sorting processes
